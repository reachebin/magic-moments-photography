About Magic Moments

Warm storytelling with an editorial eye.

Magic Moments Photography is led by Antony, Albi, and Mithun.

The work blends fine-art elegance with documentary honesty. That means helpful direction when needed, quiet observation when it matters, and a consistent focus on creating images that feel timeless rather than trendy.

Whether it is a wedding day, a couple session, a family portrait, or an event, the goal stays the same: preserve the feeling, not just the pose.

Frequently asked

Helpful answers before you inquire.

How do we book?

Send your date, session type, and location through the contact form or by email. Availability and package details will be shared as the next step.

Do you travel?

Yes. Weddings and sessions can include travel. Share the location when you inquire so it can be built into your quote clearly.

When will we receive our photos?

Sneak peeks are typically shared within 48 hours. Full gallery timing depends on the season and scope of coverage.

What if we feel awkward in front of the camera?

That is completely normal. Sessions are guided in a relaxed way so you are never left guessing what to do, but the final images still feel natural and unforced.
